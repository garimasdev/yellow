About Us

Hotel The Yellow features accommodation with a terrace, free private parking and a restaurant. This 3-star hotel offers an ATM and a concierge service. The accommodation offers a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi throughout the property.

Sukhna Lake is 4.3 km from Hotel The Yellow, while Mohali Cricket Stadium is 14 km from the property. Chandigarh Airport is 12 km away.

The banquet hall, caters to all kinds of events; such as weddings, birthday parties, conferences and live performances. The banquet hall can comfortably accommodate up to 150 people at once.
