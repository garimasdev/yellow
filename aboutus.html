About Us

Welcome to Hotel The Yellow

Your premier destination for a luxurious and convenient stay in Chandigarh. Situated in the heart of the city, our hotel offers easy access to some of the top locations that make Chandigarh a vibrant and exciting place to visit.

Location

Hotel The Yellow is strategically located, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers. Our hotel is within close proximity to several key attractions and landmarks in Chandigarh. Whether you’re visiting the prestigious PGI Hospital, exploring the academic excellence of Panjab University, admiring the art and culture at the Museum & Gallery, indulging in retail therapy at Elante Mall, strolling through the serene Rose Garden, or catching a bus to explore the city, our hotel’s location ensures that you are never far from where you need to be.

Amenities

At The Yellow, we take pride in offering premium accommodation that caters to all your needs. Our well-appointed rooms are designed to provide you with the utmost comfort and convenience during your stay.
